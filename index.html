Welcome to Pig Game!

Welcome to Pig Game! In this game, you play in turns. Each turn you press "ROLL DICE" as many times as you want. With every click, you accumulate points. If you get 1 in the dice, you lose all your accumulated points, and the turn passes to the other player. Each time you click "ROLL DICE", you can click "Hold" and keep the points you have accumulated until then, and the turn will pass to the other player. The goal: Achieve 100 points. Have Fun!
